Procure-To-Pay Features

 

More Visibility, More Savings

P2P automation saves you money! Improved insight and control over purchasing have a clear impact on your bottom line. To maximize contract benefits and prevent vendors from over charging, use Procure-to-Pay to understand your spend and help ensure contract compliance.

 

Improved Accuracy

Manual processing can be error-prone and cumbersome. With Procure-to-Pay, automation replaces manual efforts and inefficient systems for time savings, high visibility and complete control. Finance leaders increase the accuracy of their financial statements and forecasts, and operational leaders improve their staff’s vendor interactions.

 

Increased Value from Purchases

Armed with reports, analyses and key performance indicators (KPIs), you can more easily maximize your sourcing negotiations’ value and return on investment (ROI). Our Buyer Portal ensures automated and systematic purchases from only contracted vendors and supports negotiated discounts.

 

Reduced Costs and Risks

Greater procurement efficiency drives your overall costs down. You minimize the risks of vendor overcharge, maverick buying from non-contract vendors and losing discounts.

 

Efficient Collaboration

Extending your P2P process via our Supplier Portal enables suppliers to connect and collaborate with you more effectively and efficiently.

 

Seamless User Experience

Built on a common service-oriented architecture (SOA), Procure-to-Pay provides a seamless user experience throughout the P2P cycle – from requisition to purchase order (PO), PO to invoice and invoice to reimbursement.

 

Fast Launch, Low Maintenance

Procure-to-Pay is offered as a subscription-based service. With nothing to install and automatic updates, the need to rely on information technology (IT) resources diminishes sharply. If you're transitioning from a system with little to no automation, you'll like the fast ROI.

 

Easy Integration

The solution integrates easily with AP and ERP systems, improving speed, usability and performance.

 

Scalability

Our modular design enables you to select only the pieces of the procurement solution that your organization needs. The solution expands as you gain functionality.
