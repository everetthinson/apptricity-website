Apptricity to Exhibit and Sponsor Opening Reception at SCOPE FALL 2015

Global provider of mobile supply chain management and spend management solutions shares expertise at SCOPE Fall in San Diego

Dallas, Texas (PRWEB) August 20, 2015

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced its plans to exhibit and sponsor the opening reception at SCOPE Supply Chain Conference, the top executive-level event for the supply chain industry. Key Apptricity supply chain executives will be available at booth 301 to share their expertise and provide solutions for improving supply chain efficiency and performance.

Held in San Diego, CA at the Hilton San Diego Bayfront, August 23-25th, this year's SCOPE dynamic conference series promises to provide a highly educational, productive and enjoyable program for attendees. Educational sessions, networking opportunities, panels, presentations, real-world insights and case studies set the stage for learning, process improvements and emerging partnerships.

“Please be sure to stop by the Opening Welcome Cocktail Reception hosted by Apptricity because we look forward to meeting you. We will also have an educational speaking session featuring Astronaut Charlie Duke,” said Tim Garcia, Founder and CEO.

Apptricity Advisory board member, Brigadier General and Apollo 16 Astronaut, Charlie Duke, will present Apollo: Destination Moon! His presentation will include stories of his time as an astronaut as well as technology then, now and in the future.

“We are excited to be a Premier Sponsor of the leading Executive-Level Supply Chain Conference in North America. Please be sure to stop by the Opening Welcome Cocktail Reception hosted by Apptricity because we look forward to meeting you. We will also have an educational speaking session featuring Astronaut Charlie Duke,” said Tim Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity.

In addition to Charlie Dukes' presentation, Apptricity will have a booth staffed with several high-level executives who will meet with attendees, answer questions and discuss recent industry developments. SCOPE attendees are cordially invited to visit with Apptricity’s supply chain executives at booth 301.

Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, Apptricity's advanced mobile SCM solution suite applies unprecedented insight, visibility and control to complex supply chain operations that include asset tracking, inventory management, order fulfillment, warehouse management, supplier integration and more. The suite includes Apptricity Asset, Apptricity Inventory, Apptricity Work Order, Apptricity Warehouse and Apptricity Field Services. Apptricity Procure-to-Pay is also available for the integration of warehouse operations with purchasing and payment cycles.
