Automated Management of Assets Throughout Their Lifecycle

Apptricity Asset is an end-to-end enterprise asset management and tracking solution. Asset helps you effectively manage your physical assets by automating enterprise-wide asset tracking, assignments, inventory components, maintenance and valuation, extending asset life and improving organizational planning.

Asset provides management with critical, real-time data for making solid purchasing, allocation, scheduling, useful-life and salvaging decisions:

  • Along with managing individually identified items, Asset also handles the inventory of bulk items and related components, providing your organization with overall detailed visibility and tracking.
  • Asset includes a built-in maintenance scheduling component, allowing you to not only schedule and track the maintenance of your assets, but also schedule and track work done throughout your organization using the assets as tools and consumable components in the jobs.
  • Asset supports regulatory compliance with the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA), and Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) requirements.

Asset Features

 

Asset Tracking

Asset starts tracking assets when they are received and continues tracking movement and maintenance throughout their useful lifecycle and into obsolescence. Assets can also be added, transferred and assigned en masse.

 

RFID Tagging

Asset utilizes Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) tags to track asset movement; as well as, help with physical counts, letting the hardware do all the work.

 

Ongoing History

As assets are assigned and monitored, Asset provides an ongoing history of reassignments, maintenance, repairs and general usability.

 

Seamless Integration

Asset seamlessly integrates with existing ERP and GL systems; including: SAP®, Oracle®, PeopleSoft, Lawson™, Epicor®, Great Plains, QuickBooks®, MAPICS™, Microsoft Dynamics® solutions and custom legacy solutions.

 

GPS Mapping

Asset can use satellite and/or cellular networks to get precision location data that can be plotted on interactive maps providing a visual representation of global asset distribution.

 

Asset Valuation

When assets are received, detailed financial information can be gathered, including expected life. Asset provides a graphical representation of where the asset is in its lifecycle along with depreciation related information.

 

Mobile Support

Asset communicates with barcode scanners, RFID readers, and mobile devices allowing you to quickly and easily manage asset and inventory details, change locations, assignments and take inventory on the go.

  

Inventory Data

Asset provides an inventory catalog on a mobile device or through the web. Users with proper permissions can receive inventory, move it around, adjust quantities and associate them with assets as needed.
