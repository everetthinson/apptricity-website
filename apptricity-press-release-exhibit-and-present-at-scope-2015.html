Apptricity to Exhibit and Present at SCOPE 2015

Global provider of mobile supply chain management solutions shares expertise at SCOPE Spring

(PRWEB) April 23, 2015

“We look forward to the opportunity to participate in SCOPE Spring as a Premier Sponsor and to share the extensive experience we’ve gained by addressing critical industry challenges”, said Timothy D. Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity.

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced its plans to exhibit and present at SCOPE Spring 2015, the top executive-level event for the supply chain industry. Key Apptricity supply chain experts will be available at booth 201 to share their knowledge and experience and answer questions.

Held in Baltimore, Md., at the Hilton Baltimore April 26 – 28, 2015, this year’s SCOPE conference promises to provide a rich experience for attendees. A compelling and practical educational program, networking opportunities, panels, presentations, and real-world insights and case studies set the stage for learning, process improvements and emerging partnerships.

Mark Oliver, executive vice president, Apptricity, will present Adapting Your Business to the Age of Now by Improving Supply Chain Management Efficiency. His presentation will cover Supply Chain Execution Convergence (SCEC), the merging of critical supply chain elements and procedures. SCEC gives operations professionals visibility into the entire start-to-finish SCM process. Oliver will explain how new SCM technologies can increase competitiveness, how 360-degree supply chain visibility vastly improves the customer experience and how to leverage mobile devices for essential real-time information delivery.

“Supporting the supply chain industry with robust and innovative technology solutions is one of our specialties,” said Timothy D. Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity. “We look forward to the opportunity to participate in SCOPE Spring as a Premier Sponsor and to share the extensive experience we’ve gained by addressing critical industry challenges.”

In addition to Oliver’s presentation, Apptricity will have a booth staffed with several high-level experts who will meet with attendees, answer questions and discuss recent industry developments. SCOPE attendees are cordially invited to visit with Apptricity’s supply chain specialists at booth 201.

Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, Apptricity’s advanced mobile SCM solution suite applies unprecedented insight, visibility and control to complex supply chain operations that include asset tracking, inventory management, order fulfillment, warehouse management, supplier integration and more. The suite includes Apptricity Asset, Apptricity Inventory, Apptricity Work Order, Apptricity Warehouse and Apptricity Field Services. Apptricity Procure-to-Pay is also available for the integration of warehouse operations with purchasing and payment cycles.
