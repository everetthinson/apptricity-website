Automate Inventory Management for Enterprise-Wide Visibility

Apptricity Inventory enables you to manage inventory across every worksite and location in your enterprise. This cloud-based solution offers a user-friendly interface and convenient mobile capabilities for users and supervisors to easily view, manage and adjust inventory anywhere - anytime.

From receiving through consumption, easily maintain all aspects of your inventory and get detailed information regarding tracking, counting and reconciling throughout the lifecycle.

  • Obtain a 360-degree view of your inventory levels using multiple parameters, including: stock keeping unit (SKU), product type, barcode, manufacturer, supplier and facility.
  • Get complete awareness of inventory locations enterprise-wide to eliminate costly mistakes like ordering products for one location while another has plenty on hand.
  • Benefit from at-a-glance visibility while tracking products, parts on hand, supplies and materials.
  • Eliminate the need to invest in the infrastructure, people, equipment and processes required for a traditional, full-scale warehouse operation.

Inventory can be utilized as a standalone product or seamlessly integrated with Apptricity Asset (manage assets and inventory throughout your organization) or with Apptricity Work Order (track and cost out inventory used on jobs). Streamline operations at distribution centers, storage and manufacturing locations, retail stores and facilities of any size.

Inventory Features

Comprehensive Oversight

Inventory gives you a comprehensive, enterprise-wide view of all your products; including: stock quantity, location, shelf-life, and expiration information making it easy to ensure timely and accurate inventory replenishments according to company threshold limits.

Flexible Receiving Methods

Inventory leverages radio frequency (RF), Electronic Data Interface (EDI) and Advanced Shipment Notification (ASN) for real-time business intelligence on quantity, location, condition, status and other storage and shipping data.

Mobile Support

Inventory communicates with barcode scanners, smartphones and tablets, allowing you to quickly and easily manage inventory details, perform counts and adjust quantities on the go - across all your locations.

Seamless Integration

Inventory seamlessly integrates with existing ERP and GL systems like SAP®, Oracle®, PeopleSoft, Lawson®, Epicor®, and Great Plains® as well as point-of-sale, ordering applications and other legacy systems.

Automated Processes

Inventory automates processes across your entire enterprise, simplifying ordering with at-a-glance visibility, tracking and reporting. Eliminate costly labor-intensive manual processes prone to human error.

Real-Time Reporting

Receive real-time business intelligence through reporting. Managers can oversee inventory levels and trends by location, with a robust set of out-of-the-box reports.

Optimize Inventory

Reduce labor and storage costs by properly monitoring inventory levels. Inventory improves accuracy and speeds fulfillment by eliminating slowdowns due to backorders, stock outs and shortages.

Leverage Infrastructure

Inventory can utilize any data source and is built upon a proven technology framework, enabling unprecedented adaptability and user configuration capabilities. Utilize the SaaS implementation for effortless support.

Add-In Components

Inventory combined with Apptricity’s powerful Asset and Work Order modules delivers complete management and visibility of job-based asset usage MR&O process support.
