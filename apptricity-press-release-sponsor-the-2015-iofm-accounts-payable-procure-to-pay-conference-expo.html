Apptricity to Sponsor the 2015 IOFM Accounts Payable & Procure-to-Pay Conference & Expo

AP and P2P global solution provider shares mobile spend management expertise at IOFM event

(PRWEB) May 14, 2015

“Our solution portfolio is strategically structured to enhance and streamline spend management, which includes AP, P2P and the mobile applications that support them to empower the workforce,” said Timothy D. Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity.

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced that it will exhibit and present at the 2015 Accounts Payable & Procure-to-Pay (AP & P2P) Conference & Expo held by the Institute of Finance and Management (IOFM). Seasoned spend management professionals from Apptricity will be available at conference booth 512 to share their knowledge and expertise and answer questions.

Held in Orlando, Fla., at Disney’s Yacht & Beach Club Resorts® May 18 – 20, 2015, this exclusive IOFM conference will provide a rich source of relevant opportunities for education, networking, practical problem-solving and strategic development. Upwardly mobile AP professionals attend the conference for its learning environment and the chance to connect with peers and experts. Registered with the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA®), IOFM offers continuing education credits attendees can earn directly at the conference.

In its role as a silver sponsor of the event, Apptricity is providing thought leadership from Executive Vice President Mark Oliver, who will present AP Automation Project Tips for Non-Techies: Before you Buy, How to Inspect the Foundation. Oliver’s presentation covers the basics of transitioning from manual processes to automation. Using non-technical, easy-to-understand terms, Oliver emphasizes the importance of a company’s technology foundation and how the foundation relates to AP automation. Conference participants who attend Oliver’s presentation will receive continuing education credits.

“Our solution portfolio is strategically structured to enhance and streamline spend management, which includes AP, P2P and the mobile applications that support them to empower the workforce,” said Timothy D. Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity. “We provide the right information at the right time in the right place, which is the perfect fit for in an increasingly detached workforce in today’s decentralized organization.”

Numerous high-profile customers have benefited from Apptricity’s fully automated AP and P2P solutions. Apptricity Expense supplies comprehensive tracking and reporting for all types of business expenses, along with optional purchasing card (P-Card) management capabilities. Apptricity Invoice enables rapid, automated invoice processing while supplying comprehensive analytics and reporting capabilities. Apptricity Procure-to-Pay integrates Apptricity Purchasing, Apptricity Invoice and any needed Apptricity Warehouse capabilities to span the entire P2P cycle of ordering through invoicing and payment. By establishing a streamlined, consolidated process, Procure-to-Pay brings transparency, efficiency and automation to complex purchasing cycles.

Many of today’s AP and P2P challenges stem from relying on manual processes and spreadsheets. Apptricity has assisted numerous organizations by implementing automated processes that save time and money, and result in vast efficiency improvements. Other common concerns involve optimizing mobile capabilities, ensuring timely billing and establishing transparency for compliance.

Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, Apptricity’s advanced mobile Spend Management solution suite brings efficiency and enterprise-wide visibility to procurement, invoice processing, expense control and financial reporting operations while supporting regulatory compliance. Readily integrating with existing systems, the suite includes Expense, Invoice, Procure-to-Pay, Purchasing and Apptricity Time and Attendance.
