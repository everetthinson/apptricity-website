Apptricity to Sponsor the Argyle Executive 2015 Chief Financial Officer Leadership Forum

Global provider of mobile spend management and supply chain management will attend strategic invitation only event

Dallas, Texas (PRWEB) September 16, 2015

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced that it will sponsor the 2015 Chief Financial Officer Leadership Forum in Dallas on September 17, 2015. Apptricity leaders and account executives will be attending the event to network and share their knowledge and expertise.

“Apptricity is excited about being part of the Chief Financial Officer Leadership Forum. We feel our products are strategic and provide tremendous value add to the participants of this event," said Tim Garcia, Founder and CEO, Apptricity.

The invitation only event held in Dallas, Texas will bring together CFOs, Controllers and other finance leaders to focus on several areas including effective strategies for sustainable growth and innovation, cost leadership, risk mitigation, fraud management and more.

Apptricity is a Networking Partner of the event, sponsoring breakfast and introducing a key speaker. Tim Garcia, founder and CEO, Apptricity will provide a brief overview of Apptricity. He will also introduce speaker Doug Herron, CFO, Safelite AutoGlass who will be presenting a case study entitled “Driving Business Performance through Cultural Choices”.

Apptricity will host a drawing for attendees at the Apptricity table to win an official NFL Dallas Cowboys football autographed by Tony Dorsett, former Dallas Cowboys running back, Heisman Trophy winner and Hall of Famer.

“Apptricity is excited about being part of the Chief Financial Officer Leadership Forum. We feel our products are strategic and provide tremendous value add to the participants of this event,” said Tim Garcia.

Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, Apptricity’s mobile Spend Management portfolio includes Apptricity Travel and Expense, Apptricity Invoice, Apptricity Purchasing, Apptricity Time and Attendance, and Apptricity Procure-to Pay. Apptricity also offers SCM SaaS solutions to manage complex supply cycles. By integrating Integrate finance-related functions with critical supply chain operations, clients can automate their entire enterprise.
