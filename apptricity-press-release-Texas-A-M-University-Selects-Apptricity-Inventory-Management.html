Texas A&M University Selects Apptricity Inventory Management to Administer Corps of Cadets Uniforms

RFID technology helps automate and manage uniform inventory

Dallas, Texas (PRWEB) October 27, 2015

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced that Texas A&M University selected Apptricity for its RFID based Inventory Management solution.

“ We are pleased that Texas A&M University selected Apptricity Inventory Management to automate and better manage uniform inventory, issuance, returns and billing,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity President and CEO.

Texas A&M University is very proud of its nationally famous Fightin' Texas Aggie Band and is one of the largest programs in the country. Apptricity was selected through a RFP process to provide an Inventory Management System for the Military Property Warehouse located on the College Station campus.

Apptricity will be providing a RFID based approach to Inventory Management for the Military Property Warehouse (MPW). The MPW stores and distributes uniforms for the Corps of Cadets. The full uniform cycle spans the freshman through senior year. Apptricity will be addressing possible tag replacement for current uniform expendable items using existing bar codes, as well as current uniform returnable and re-issuable items using existing RFID chips.

Apptricity will be providing a cut over plan for existing uniforms and will include hardware installation to include RFID chip installation, centralized software development and implementation provisions and updates, staff training and the handling of early-stage support calls.

“The Corps of Cadets is renowned and a tradition in the State of Texas and the USA. We are pleased to be working with Texas A&M University to help them reduce the of time and resources managing all the uniforms,” said Tim Garcia, Apptricity President and CEO. “We are pleased that Texas A&M University selected Apptricity Inventory Management to automate and better manage uniform inventory, issuance, returns and billing. Our solution will provide real-time visibility to better manage uniform assets while giving the Cadets a better experience.”
