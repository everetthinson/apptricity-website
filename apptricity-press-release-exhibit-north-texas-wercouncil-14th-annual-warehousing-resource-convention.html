Apptricity to Exhibit at the North Texas WERCouncil 14th Annual Warehousing Resource Convention

Global provider of mobile supply chain management and spend management solutions will exhibit at North Texas event for logistics and distribution professionals

Dallas, Texas (PRWEB) October 8, 2015

Apptricity® Corporation, a leading global provider of innovative mobile enterprise solutions for supply chain management (SCM) and spend management, today announced that it will exhibit at the North Texas WERCouncil 14th Annual Warehousing Resource Convention in Grapevine, Texas on October 13, 2015.

“Apptricity is excited to support the North Texas WERCouncil with our sponsorship and to exhibit our enterprise supply chain management solutions; including, Apptricity Warehouse”, said Diane Mitchell, VP Marketing & Sales, Apptricity.

Apptricity account executives will be attending the event to network and share their knowledge and expertise in mobile supply chain and warehouse management. As an added bonus for attendees, Apptricity will host a drawing at the Apptricity table to win an official NFL Dallas Cowboys football autographed by Tony Dorsett, former Dallas Cowboys running back, Heisman Trophy winner and Hall of Famer.

In its 14th year, this event has provided a great educational and networking value for attendees, especially those living in North Texas. The one-day event held at the Grapevine Convention Center will bring together logistics, warehouse, distribution and supply chain professionals for the opportunity to make local resource connections, have valuable one-on-one time with suppliers, and network with peers.

“Apptricity is excited to support the North Texas WERCouncil with our sponsorship and to exhibit our enterprise supply chain management solutions; including, Apptricity Warehouse”, said Diane Mitchell, VP Marketing & Sales, Apptricity.

Powered by Apptricity JetStream platform, Apptricity’s mobile enterprise supply chain management portfolio includes Apptricity Warehouse, Apptricity Asset, Apptricity Inventory, Apptricity Work Order, and Apptricity Field Services. Apptricity also offers spend management SaaS solutions. By integrating finance-related functions with critical supply chain operations, clients can automate their entire enterprise.

About Warehousing Education and Research Council (WERC)

WERC offers resources that help distribution professionals stay at the leading edge including educational events, performance metrics for benchmarking, practical research, expert insights and peer-to-peer knowledge exchange. WERC members are from companies across the gamut whose focus is on warehousing and distribution. For more information, visit http://www.werc.org.
